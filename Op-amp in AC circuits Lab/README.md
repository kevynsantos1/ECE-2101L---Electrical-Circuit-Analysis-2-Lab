In this lab, we will be analyzing the characteristics of an operational amplifier within different AC
circuits. We will calculate the RMS (root mean squared) values of our source and initial voltages
prior to measuring so that we have a baseline for what our data should look like. We will also
review the datasheet of our operational amplifier, LM741, so that we know how to build our
circuits properly. In the second half of the lab, we will analyze how a resistor and capacitor in
parallel affect our operational amplifier circuit.
