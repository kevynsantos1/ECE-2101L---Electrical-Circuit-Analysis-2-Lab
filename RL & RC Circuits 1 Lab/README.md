In this lab, we will examine the voltage and current relationships of parallel RL and RC circuits.
Since we need to use Kirchhoff’s Current Law to analyze parallel circuits we need to know the
differences and similarities between parallel phasor circuits compared to serial phasor circuits
and parallel DC circuits. When it comes to phase, we know that the voltage and current of
resistors are in phase, capacitors lag by 90 degrees and inductors lead by 90 degrees. We will
also use a different technique for measuring the currents of our circuit branches since the
oscilloscope is unable to directly measure them.
